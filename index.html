Mouse & Keyboard

Plug & Play

Turn your screen into a trackpad.


  • Move: Slide one finger.
  • Click: Tap once (Left click).
  • Right Click: Tap with two fingers.
  • Scroll: Drag with two fingers.

Second Monitor

Driver Req

Extend your desktop workspace to your Android device.


  • Step 1: Download & Extract usbmmidd_v2 (link above).
  • Step 2: Open Command Prompt as Admin in that folder.
  • Step 3: Run:
    deviceinstaller64 install usbmmidd.inf usbmmidd
  • Step 4: On PC: Settings > System > Display > Extend these displays.
  • Step 5: On Phone: Open Monitor tab.

Gamepad (Xbox/PS)

No Setup

Play PC games using your phone as a controller.


  • Layouts: Toggle between Xbox and PlayStation layouts in the app.
  • Haptics: Turn on/off Vibration using the vibration button.
  • Compatibility: Works with Steam, Epic Games, and Emulators.

Microphone

VB-Cable Req

Use your phone as a high-quality PC mic.


  • Step 1: Install VB-Cable driver.
  • Step 2: Open Use As app > Click Microphone.
  • Step 3: In Zoom/Discord, set Input Device to "CABLE Output".

Webcam

Driver Req

Stream phone camera to PC for meetings or streaming.


  • Step 1: Install Unity Capture driver (see Downloads).
  • Step 2: In the Server App, open the Camera tab.
  • Step 3: In Zoom/OBS, select "Unity Video Capture" as the source.

PC Speaker

Stereo Mix

Listen to PC audio on your phone wirelessly.


  • Setup: Enable "Stereo Mix" in Windows Sound Settings.
  • Usage: Open Speaker tab on phone > Tap Toggle.
  • Auto-Stop: Streaming stops automatically when you close the app to save battery.

File Transfer

Easy

Send photos, videos, or text between devices.


  • Phone to PC: Select file in app > Send. Saves to Downloads/UseAs_Received.
  • PC to Phone: Drag & Drop file onto the Server Window.
  • Clipboard: Copy text on one device, it auto-copies to the other.
